Ingredients:
 - 4 avocados
 - 4 tomatos
 - 4 spoons of olive oil
 - 1 spoon of soja sauce

Directions:
  1. Prepare the avocados, cut them in big enough pieces; we don't want them to turn into a paste.
  2. Cut the tomatos in medium cubes.
  3. Add the olive oil and soja sauce and mix everything together.
  4. Serve fresh
